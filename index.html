About

Boston Outside Film Festival is a child project of Watch It Outside. The company just got the approval to show movies in Boston Public Garden this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight. We can't wait to help the public discover awesome films!

Festival News Update

3 Additional Big Screens Added
July 7th, 2019

All hands has continuously been on deck since Boston Garden was declared as the place where this year's edition will be held. In order to enhance viewer's experience, 3 outdoor screen was purchased to this effect. The purchase was made possible through the help of partners.

Free Popcorn and Chapman every night
July 3rd, 2019

The Chairman of the Film Festival last night declared that he had released funds which will adequately cater for the stomach infrastructure of all attendees during the Film Festival. Sources have it that Popcorn and Chapman tops the list of edibles currently under considerations to be served at the festival. This will make the Film Festival deliver best and memorable experience to attendees.

Security in top shape
July 1st, 2019

As 2019 edition of the annual film festival for this year draws near, we have dimmed it fit to reach out to the security chiefs and organization saddled with providing the neighbourhood with security services collaborate with us throughout the days of the Film Festival. The response came in today through a letter signed by the company's PR and Communications Manager making known the organization's desire to provide full security services during each day of the festival.

Stage Lightening Purchase Delivered
June 30th, 2019

Who else needs to here this and sure would be excited. It is no longer news that we justed added 2 extra lightenings (DMX Analogue Dimmers and Color Contollers) to our gear's list and also enhance the ambience at the festival. Excited about this, then done miss the Film Festival for any reason because i am very sure that you won't forgive yourself if you do so.

Boston Rap Artist Confirmed Attendance
June 29th, 2019

Top of the season shoutout to everyone. The entire planning committee of the Boston's Film Festival is pleased to announce to you that popular Boston Rap Artist in person of James Walker just officially declared through his twitter handle this afternoon that he would be attending of session of the Film Festival.

A list movies approved for the Festival
June 20th, 2019

The recent approval from the national executives of the Arts and cinematography Association of Boston to air the A-List movie categories for this year's edition of our Film Festival has added more joy and energy among the organizers. This means participants will have the priviledge to see movies in this category for free. You won't want to be left out of this.
